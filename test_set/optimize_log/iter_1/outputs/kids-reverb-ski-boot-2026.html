Comfort meets performance. Based on our high-tech high-comfort BFC line of adult boots the Reverb is a unisex boot that offers a comfy accommodating fit and a supportive flex for your growing ski star. The K2 Reverb Youth Ski Boots 2026 provide confidence for the next generation of skiers. These intermediate-level all mountain boots feature a 2-piece construction with PP shell and cuff materials. The boots include a Cushfit Comfort liner with additional foam in key areas for increased comfort and compliance, along with GripWalk outsole technology for better walking comfort and top skiing performance.
