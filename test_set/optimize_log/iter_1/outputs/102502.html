Designed for movement, with perfect functionality and a focus on breathability and strength, the Aurion 3L pants, made with the Toray Dermizax 20K / 20K membrane, invite you to explore the mountains. Highly breathable and stretchy, with a wide cut, long vents, small pockets, adjustable belt, and Cordura® ankle reinforcement around an integrated extra water-resistant snow gaiter, all you have to do is choose your destination and buckle up.

Made in Japan, the Toray Dermizax™ membrane is ultra-thin, breathable, stretchable, PFAS-free, and blocks wind and water for comfort and freedom of movement.

Features

  • Built-in adjustable belt for secure customizable fit
  • YKK aquaguard 2-way long side zip venting system
  • Zip thigh pockets with phone/beacon pocket inside
  • Cordura® hem reinforcements for enhanced durability
  • Internal boot gaiter with extra water-resistant protection
  • Recco reflector technology for emergency rescue situations
