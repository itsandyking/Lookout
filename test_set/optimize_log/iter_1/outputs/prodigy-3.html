The Prodigy 3 is designed for supreme all-mountain enjoyment and durability in any conditions, making it ideal for both packed and powder snow. This intuitive, perfectly balanced, and stable ski features a 106 mm waist, enhancing your experience in nature's finest playground. Its Directional Twin shape and Elliptical Sidecut ensure user-friendly performance, suited for avid skiers looking to progress their skills across all the mountain has to offer. The Prodigy 3 belongs in your collection as an all-mountain quiver-killer ski, retaining the playful character the Prodigy Series is famous for. A considerably skinnier tail width than in the tip provides downhill performance for attacking the mountain, while a twin tip in the tail encourages playfulness and freestyle attitude, meaning you can ride switch with ease.

Features

  • Playful ski for all conditions, including powder snow
  • Carves hard, but also easily pivots and smears
  • Directional twin shape with twin tip for switch riding
  • Elliptical sidecut ensures user-friendly performance across mountain terrain
  • 106mm waist width for enhanced all-mountain experience
