The MTN Surf Recycled Pants are stormproof shred pants made with a body mapped 100% recycled fabric layout that is as comfortable as your favorite jeans. They are lined with 100% recycled stretch polyester for a little extra warmth and offer the perfect balance of mobility, breathability and durability. Ideal for every day resort and backcountry shredding, these lined pants provide all-conditions warmth and durability with supreme comfort and relaxed fit.

Features

  • Two zipped thigh pockets with internal phone sleeves
  • Durable powder gaiters with reinforced inner cuff fabric
  • YKK Aquaguard and Natulon recycled zippers throughout
  • Fully taped seams for complete weather protection
  • Adjustable waist with belt loops and double snap closure
  • Side seam vents for temperature regulation
