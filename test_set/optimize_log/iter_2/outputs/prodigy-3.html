The Prodigy 3 is designed for supreme all-mountain enjoyment and durability in any conditions, making it ideal for both packed and powder snow. This intuitive, perfectly balanced, and stable ski features a 106 mm waist, enhancing your experience in nature's finest playground. Its Directional Twin shape and Elliptical Sidecut ensure user-friendly performance, suited for avid skiers looking to progress their skills across all the mountain has to offer. The Prodigy 3 belongs in your collection as an all-mountain quiver-killer ski, retaining the playful character the Prodigy Series is famous for.

Features

  • Directional Twin shape with twin tip for switch riding
  • 106 mm waist width for all-mountain versatility
  • Elliptical Sidecut provides user-friendly carving performance
  • Poplar Core construction with Mustache Flex technology
  • XL 2.5mm Edges with Full Strength Sidewall construction
  • Anti-Chip Micro-Cap and recycled materials integration
