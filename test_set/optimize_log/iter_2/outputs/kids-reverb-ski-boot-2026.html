Comfort meets performance. Based on our high-tech high-comfort BFC line of adult boots the Reverb is a unisex boot that offers a comfy accommodating fit and a supportive flex for your growing ski star. The sole-binding system provides better walking comfort and top skiing performance. GripWalk contains of a new co-polymer sole with rocker profile. The convex shape and ribbed tread of the rubber add a lot of walking comfort and provide a much better grip. Both the boot's shell and cuff are capable of being heat-molded to a variety of foot shapes for a true custom fit.

Features

  • PP shell provides lightweight forgiving plastic for easy flexing
  • Polypropylene cuff delivers consistent flex and reliable support conditions
  • Cushfit Comfort liner with additional foam in key areas
  • 30mm Velcro strap offers lightweight user-friendly secure closure
  • Fully heat-moldable shell and cuff for true custom fit
  • Hands free entry with dual material overlap design
