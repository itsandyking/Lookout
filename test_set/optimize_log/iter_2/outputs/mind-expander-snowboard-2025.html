The Mind Expander is a playful all-mountain board designed by surf shaper Chris Christenson that wants to pop pillows and porpoise through pow. It's a friendly ride as it's easy to turn and has a directional shape with a long nose and tapered tail for epic float. This is a board for the creative rider who likes to blaze fresh tracks and draw playful lines down the mountain. Alternative all-mountain shredder ideal for creative freeriding and freestyle. Friendly surf slasher, easy-to-turn, floaty design for versatile mountain riding.

Features

  • Easy to turn with friendly ride characteristics
