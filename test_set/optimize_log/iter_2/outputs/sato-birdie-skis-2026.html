A refined new model designed for the piste and its surroundings; the Sato is a ski bursting with joy. Precise, playful and eye-catching, this small ski loves to carve on immaculate slopes, have fun with the terrain or offer a few sessions between runs. Future resort star, the new Sato is a ski full of momentum that will delight those who like to spin along the slopes and roam the domains. With its median flex, it is playful and adapts easily to everyone's style, but it's also edge-holding and incisive for carving at speed. Its reasonable but consistent width makes it an all-terrain ski that can tame wild snow.

Features

  • Intermediate floatation with 88mm waist width for versatility
  • Progressive front rocker and early rise tail for control
  • Slightly short sidecut provides good carving and reactivity
  • Tolerant progressive flex comfortable in curves and all terrain
  • Crystal-clear ski designed for joyful mountain experiences
