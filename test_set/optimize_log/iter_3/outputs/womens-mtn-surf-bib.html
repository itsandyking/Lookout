The MTN Surf Recycled Pants are stormproof shred pants made with a body mapped 100% recycled fabric layout that is as comfortable as your favorite jeans. They are lined with 100% recycled stretch polyester for a little extra warmth and offer the perfect balance of mobility, breathability and durability. Ideal for every day resort and backcountry shredding, these lined pants provide supreme comfort with a relaxed fit for all-conditions warmth and durability.

Features

  • Body mapped 100% recycled fabric construction
  • Lined with 100% recycled stretch polyester
  • Two zipped thigh pockets with internal phone sleeves
  • Durable powder gaiters and reinforced inner cuff fabric
  • YKK Aquaguard and Natulon recycled zippers throughout
  • Fully taped seams for complete weather protection
