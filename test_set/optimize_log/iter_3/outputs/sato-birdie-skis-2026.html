A refined new model designed for the piste and its surroundings; the Sato is a ski bursting with joy. Precise, playful and eye-catching, this small ski loves to carve on immaculate slopes, have fun with the terrain or offer a few sessions between runs. A crystal-clear ski, a joyful ski, a blackcrows ski. Future resort star, the new Sato is a ski full of momentum that will delight those who like to spin along the slopes and roam the domains. With its median flex, it is playful and adapts easily to everyone's style, but it's also edge-holding and incisive for carving at speed. Its reasonable but consistent width makes it an all-terrain ski that can tame wild snow. A freedom-loving ski to ignite your sensual curves.

Features

  • Median flex design for playful and adaptable performance
  • Edge-holding and incisive construction for high-speed carving
  • All-terrain ski capable of handling wild snow conditions
  • Designed specifically for piste and surrounding terrain exploration
  • Reasonable but consistent width for versatile mountain performance
