A smaller, lighter version in our ProTac series. This rope is manufactured from solution dyed polyester for excellent durability. The 32 carrier sheath is designed to hold its cross section to prevent flattening and to run smoothly in descent devices. The design incorporates a 50/50 ratio of sheath to core for design integrity and long life. Certified as EN 1891 Type B Low Elongation Rope. Available in 200ft, 300ft, 600ft, and 200M lengths in solid black with olive drab available upon request.

Features

  • Solution dyed polyester construction for excellent durability
  • 32 carrier sheath prevents flattening in descent devices
  • 50/50 sheath to core ratio for design integrity
  • EN 1891 Type B Low Elongation Rope certified
  • Runs smoothly in descent devices during operation
  • Available in solid black and olive drab colors
