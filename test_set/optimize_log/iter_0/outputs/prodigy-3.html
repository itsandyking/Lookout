The Prodigy 3 is designed for supreme all-mountain enjoyment and durability in any conditions, making it ideal for both packed and powder snow. This intuitive, perfectly balanced, and stable ski features a 106 mm waist, enhancing your experience in nature's finest playground. The Prodigy 3 belongs in your collection as an all-mountain quiver-killer ski, retaining the playful character the Prodigy Series is famous for.
