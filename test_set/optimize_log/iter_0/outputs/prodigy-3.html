The Prodigy 3 is designed for supreme all-mountain enjoyment and durability in any conditions, making it ideal for both packed and powder snow. This intuitive, perfectly balanced, and stable ski features a 106 mm waist, enhancing your experience in nature's finest playground. Its Directional Twin shape and Elliptical Sidecut ensure user-friendly performance, suited for avid skiers looking to progress their skills across all the mountain has to offer.

The Prodigy 3 belongs in your collection as an all-mountain quiver-killer ski, retaining the playful character the Prodigy Series is famous for.

You'll love this ski if...

  • You want a playful ski for all conditions, including powder
  • You want a ski that carves hard, but also easily pivots and smears
  • You love to butter and occasionally ski or land backwards
  • You are always on the look-out for the little pocket of pow that didnt get skied
