A refined new model designed for the piste and its surroundings; the Sato is a ski bursting with joy. Precise, playful and eye-catching, this small ski loves to carve on immaculate slopes, have fun with the terrain or offer a few sessions between runs. Future resort star, the new Sato is a ski full of momentum that will delight those who like to spin along the slopes and roam the domains. With its median flex, it is playful and adapts easily to everyone's style, but it's also edge-holding and incisive for carving at speed.
