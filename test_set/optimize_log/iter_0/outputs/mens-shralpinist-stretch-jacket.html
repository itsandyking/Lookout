Quick Tension Tail Clips are a lightweight tail clip designed to fit all 2017 and newer splitboards. The tail clips adjust to length to allow for the perfect skin tension for your splitboard size. Tail clips must be installed on skins. This skins accessory fits only Jones splitboards and includes 2x rubber extension straps, 8x rivets, and 2x Quick Tension tail clips.
