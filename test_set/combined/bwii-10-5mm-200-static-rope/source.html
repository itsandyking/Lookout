10.5mm Protac™

A smaller, lighter version in our ProTac series. This rope is manufactured from solution dyed polyester for excellent durability. The 32 carrier sheath is designed to hold its cross section to prevent flattening and to run smoothly in descent devices. The design incorporates a 50/50 ratio of sheath to core for design integrity and long life.

Certified as EN 1891 Type B Low Elongation Rope

Available in:
200ft, 300ft, 600ft, and 200M Lengths in Solid Black. Olive Drab available request.

Elongation:
@ 300 lbf = 2.6%
@ 600 lbf = 5.7%
@ 1000 lbf = 7.7%

Diameter:    10.4mm
Tensile Strength: 7,081 lbf (31.5 kN)
Grams Per Meter:    72.03
Sheath Mass:    50%
